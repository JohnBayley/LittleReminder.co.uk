Privacy Policy


This privacy policy sets out how Little Reminder uses and protects any information that you give Little Reminder when you use this website.


Little Reminder is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. We ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.


Little Reminder may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page from time to time to ensure that you are happy with any changes. This policy is effective from 17th September 2007.


What we collect

We collect the following information:

  • Your name.
  • contact information in the form of your email address.
  • demographic information such as postcode.
  • dates on events that you wish to be reminded of.

What we do with the information we gather

We require this information to carry out the reminder functions for the following reasons:

  • Your name: Used solely to personalise the experience. The name is not checked and can be a nick name or alias
  • Your email address: Used to identify you and to send you reminder emails.
  • Your postcode: This is optional and is used to provide suggestions from your local area for services that you need.
  • Dates: An automated system sends reminders about the event.

Internal record keeping.

We may use the information to improve our products and services.

We may periodically send emails about Little Reminder updates other information which are of an urgent nature such as system outages using email address which you have provided.

We do not use your information to contact you for sales, advertising or market research purposes unless it is a part of the reminder process. The pre arranged reminder emails may contain sales or advertising information or links. We will not contact you by phone, fax or mail.

Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

How we use cookies

A cookie is a small file which is placed on your computer's hard drive. As the site is highly specific to the user the cookie is necessary to log you in to the site.

As the site requires that cookies are used, we have chosen to use session cookies only. This means that there is no tracking of your use or visits to the site and the cookie is deleted when you quit your browser.

Little Reminder does not use cookies to track, monitor or target you in any way.

We will not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties unless we have your permission or are required by law to do so.

We may use your personal information to send you promotional information about third parties which we think you may find interesting.

You may request details of personal information which we hold about you under the Data Protection Act 1998. A small fee will be payable. If you would like a copy of the information held on you please email us at dpa@littlereminder.co.uk.

If you believe that any information we are holding on you is incorrect or incomplete, please email us as soon as possible at the above address. We will promptly correct any information found to be incorrect.
