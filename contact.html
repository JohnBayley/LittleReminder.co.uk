What is it

Littlereminder.co.uk provides a free reminder service. It sends you reminders via email a month, fortnight and a week before the event is due. After all everybody needs a little reminder sometime.


Who's Site is it?

Littlereminder.co.uk was devised, designed, created, programmed and is maintained by John Bayley an independent web developer based in the UK. john.bayley@littlereminder.co.uk


I don't want emails

If you have received reminders emails and wish to stop simply delete, disable or archive the reminders saved in your account.

Otherwise email reminders@littlereminder.co.uk and your reminders will be disabled for you.


Send Reminders about my Business
