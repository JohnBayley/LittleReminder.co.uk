How to use Littlereminder

Littlereminder.co.uk Help Page

1.Register for your free reminder account.

Register using the Register link. You must fill in the required fields username, password and email address to register.
You must provide a valid email address. This is because the reminders are sent via email. We do not and will not disclose your email address to any third parties.

2.Confirm your email address.

Once you have registered we will send a confirmation email to the address you supplied during registration.
Click the link in the email to activate your account. If you deleted, lost or didn't receive the confirmation email you can Request Another

3.Login.

Login by entering the username and password or your email and password at the login screen. Logging on is required to ensure that you are shown your own details and to protect your details.

4.Create your reminders.

Create your reminders by either :-
  1. Dragging from the New Reminder panel and dropping on a date.
  2. Double clicking on a date to open the new reminder dialogue.

You will receive an acknowledgement email automatically after adding a new reminder or moving a reminder to a different date.

5.Wait for your reminders.

You will then receive reminders

  • One Month before the due date
  • Two weeks before the due date
  • One week before the due date
  • The day before the due date
  • and finally on the due date
