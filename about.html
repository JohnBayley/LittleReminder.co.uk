About Little Reminder

Littlereminder.co.uk provides a free reminder service.

It sends you reminders via email a month, fortnight and a week before the event is due.

After all everybody needs a little reminder sometime.

Use it to give you reminders about

  • Birthdays
  • Anniversary
  • Car Insurance
  • House Insurance
  • MOT

Littlereminder.co.uk was devised and is maintained by John Bayley. email john.bayley@littlereminder.co.uk

What it is not

This site is not designed to obtain email adresses for spam or any form of unsolicited contact. We treat privacy very seriously and are ourselves not fans of junk mail.

As the reminders are sent by email then you must provide a valid email address to use this service.

The site may advertise in webpages or in reminder emails to provide targetted advertising services. For example, if your MOT is due we may provide details of garage services. Advertising is the way that we make money to maintain this site, however we do not and will not disclose your details to these comanies or organisations.

If you have used and given your details to a company or organisation using this service we may send emails on their behalf.

At all stages there is the opportunity to cancel emails. You may also contact us at reminders@littlereminder.co.uk and we can remove you from the system.
